About

Mental health impacts all age groups, yet identifying issues in children can be challenging, especially as they often prefer expressing themselves online. With advanced language analysis models like ChatGPT, we can now analyze conversations more effectively than ever before. To address the lack of time and patience among adults to listen to children, we propose a web application. This app would integrate ChatGPT's API to assess children's moods and provide personalized suggestions after each interaction. By leveraging technology, we aim to create a supportive platform that helps children express themselves and receive the support they need.
